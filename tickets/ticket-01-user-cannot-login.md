# Ticket 01: User Cannot Log In

## Scenario
A test user, Taylor Morgan, reported that they could not log in to their company account.

## User Request
Taylor Morgan submitted a ticket stating that they were unable to log in to their company account after several password attempts. The user needed access restored so they could begin work for the day.

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Confirmed the user's reported login issue.
2. Opened the ticket in the osTicket staff panel.
3. Added an internal note documenting the initial triage plan.
4. Documented likely next checks: account status, possible lockout, and whether a password reset was needed.
5. Posted a customer-facing reply with next steps.
6. Resolved the ticket after documenting the support response.

## Resolution
The ticket was handled as a common login/password issue. The user was advised to reset their password and attempt to sign in again. The ticket was documented with an internal triage note and a customer-facing resolution response.
